About Us

Aybie's Security is a platform where knowledge seeking individuals can learn more about the importance of Information security and its need in the present technological age. It's for everyone whether they do have an IT background or a complete laymann in terms of IT education. In the present, we are going through summit of technological advancement in the field of computer science. It doesn't matter from which sector you belong, we all use smartphones and plenty of gadgets for our daily use. It's high time for individuals of Pakistan to identify the importance of the data they possess and its safeguarding measures. Aybie's Security will not only provide learning resources for all types of digital world users but will also allow young motivated individuals to join our team and research in the domain of Information Security and make Pakistan cyber aware.

Why Information Security for Pakistan?

Pakistan is ranked one of the least cyber secure countries with the following measures.

  • 25 percent of the mobile phones are infected with malware in the form of games downloaded by children and third party applications for entertainment.
  • One of the worst prepared countries in case of a cyber attack as the nation itself has not even a single Cyber Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT).
  • 70% of the population is untrained on how to ensure security of their data on their digital devices


Effects of Poor Cyber Awareness

It is to be known that the next war is not going to be fought on battlegrounds but it will be a cyber war because data has became the most important asset now and is enough to bring down a country to its disastrous epilogue.

Each data breach does not only cause economical loss but also time loss, resource usability loss and most importantly the loss of data. Data is important to every user, whether you are a student or being a corporate. Right now 18,000+ Cases are pending in FIA for cyber-harassment, hacking, leak of personal pictures and cyberextortion. Unfortunately cyber policies are unable to recover the damage caused. There's only one way out now. Awareness!!

I don't know what Cyber security is and why is it a big deal for me?

Cyber attack is very different from a physical attack. In a physical attack if you get robbed, you instantly get to know because that thing goes missing. In a cyber attack, you won't be able to know that you are hacked unless you see your data on any public forum or the attacker reveals it to you. This is a fact that you are most likely to be a victim of a cyber theft, you just don't know it yet.
